Mocha coffee recipe

What is Mocha Coffee

Mocha is a sweet, chocolatey and sometimes creamy drink. Mocha is often made differently across the world. A basic template is a shot of espresso with a chocolate powder or syrup, followed by milk or cream. It is a latte varient, and as a result often has 1/3 espresso and 2/3 steamed milk. However, a chocolate flavour is added, and this can be milk or dark.

Ingredients

This recipe is a quick and easy drink using Keurig pods. I'm specifically using Mcdonald's "McCafe Iced Mocha Frappe" pod So this requires a Coffee machine that using Keurig pods. I don't like to handhold so the measurement are to your liking. Use you creamer of choice as well.

  • Mocha Kuerig Pod
  • Water
  • Heavy cream
  • Creamer of choice: Starbucks caramel macchiato

Steps

  1. Load your Keurig pod into the machine
  2. Select your desired cup size/water level
  3. Put your cup underneath with the desired amoujnt of ice
  4. *Press Start*
  5. After the machine is done and the cup is full then add heavy cream and creamer.
